About

LaundryLott is a mobile web app designed to make the laundry process as easy as possible.

In many colleges a recurring problem in dorm halls is overflowing laundry rooms, caused by students not getting laundry on time or machine outages.

With LaundryLott, students are incentivized to get their laundry on time while providing them with helpful info on machine status and outages, saving time and effort.
